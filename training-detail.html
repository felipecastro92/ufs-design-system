Professional Development

Course content

8 episodes
01 Understanding career pathways
Discover how you must constantly challenge yourself in the kitchen if you want to get ahead. From learning new techniques to dealing with awkward bosses.
02 Training, skills & knowledge
Get the inside track on what head chefs look for when employing a new kitchen team member. Impressive qualifications are great, but there's much more to it than that.
03 Leadership in the kitchen
Great kitchens run on trust and clear communication. Learn how the best leaders motivate teams under pressure and build lasting kitchen culture.
04 Food safety & regulatory compliance
Being a commis chef takes more than technical skills — interpersonal skills and good habits make all the difference. Learn what's expected in a professional kitchen.
05 Sustainable practices & ethical sourcing
Kitchens have a significant environmental footprint. Explore how leading chefs are reducing waste and building relationships with ethical suppliers.
06 Customer relations & service excellence
The guest experience extends far beyond the plate. Understand how back-of-house decisions directly impact front-of-house success.
07 Financial management for kitchen leaders
Understanding food cost, margins, and budgeting is essential for any ambitious chef. This episode demystifies the numbers behind a profitable kitchen.
08 Building your personal brand as a chef
In today's connected world, your reputation extends beyond the kitchen. Learn how social media, networking, and storytelling can accelerate your career.

Meet the instructor

Chef Thais Gimenez

Executive Chef & Senior Culinary Leader

Chef Thais is passionate about pastry, baking, and culinary trends. As the writer of a gastronomy newsletter, she always shares her experiences and recipes on her social media — especially dessert recipes. One of her mottos: "Difficult times call for furious desserts."
